    Our Story

    We started Cosplay Rental PH because we saw a problem: amazing cosplays sitting in closets after one convention, while new cosplayers struggled to afford their first costume. We created a platform where verified cosplay owners can monetize their collections and renters can experience the joy of cosplay without the hefty price tag.

    Security First: All owners are manually verified by our admin team before they can list costumes.

    Community First

    Cosplay is for everyone, regardless of budget.

    FAQs

    How do I pay?

    GCash, PayMaya, or cash on pickup – arranged directly with owner.

    How to become an owner?

    Sign up as owner, wait for admin approval, then list costumes.

    What if costume is damaged?

    Security deposit covers minor damages; discuss terms with owner.
